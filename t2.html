Tribute to Nani

Nani is a talented actor who works primarily in Telugu cinema. He was born on February 24, 1984, in Hyderabad, Andhra pradesh(present telangana). He made his acting debut in 2008 with the film "Ashta chamma".

Nani rose to fame with his performance in the film "Eega" in 2008. He has since acted in several successful films, including "yeto vellipoyindhi manasu", "Ninnu kori", and "Dasara".

Nani has won several awards for his acting, including the Filmfare Award for Best Actor - Telugu for "Bhale bhale magadivoy".

Nani is known for his dedication to his craft and his commitment to his fans. He is married to Anjana Yelavarthy, and he have a boy child named Arjun.
